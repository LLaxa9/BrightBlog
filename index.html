Bio

Read time: 5 minutes. Gazing at paintings: inestimable.

AA, painter

Born in 1956 in Romania, the artist’s journey is a testament to resilience and passion. His diverse career path includes roles as an accountant, administrator, naval pilot officer, canteen head, and guardsman. These experiences have shaped his unique perspective and drive to push himself and inspire others to achieve their best.

After retirement due to illness,his passion for creativity never waned. He attended a painting course at "Dunarea de Jos" Cultural Center’s Art School, where he honed his artistic skills.

Today, his paintings have captivated art aficionados in a couple European countries, reflecting his rich life experiences and unwavering passion for art.

latest update: 24-09-2024

Artist statement

Oil painting allows me to unleash my raw emotions onto the canvas, 17 years spent outdoors in the Danube Delta, and my experience as a sailor reaching the vibrant streets of India to the lush landscapes of Brazil have enriched my palette with diverse colours and textures. Through continuous experimentation and speedy execution I strive to capture the essence of my inner world and display nature’s grace.
