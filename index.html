Bio

Read time: 5 minutes. Gazing at paintings: inestimable.

Born in 1956 in Romania, the artist’s journey is a testament to resilience and passion. His diverse career path includes roles as an accountant, administrator, naval pilot officer, canteen head, and guardsman. These experiences have shaped his unique perspective and drive to push himself and inspire others to achieve their best.

After living in Germany for three years with his wife and two children

After retirement due to illness,his passion for creativity never waned. He attended a painting course at "Dunarea de Jos" Cultural Center’s Art School, where he honed his artistic skills.

Today, his paintings have captivated art aficionados in several European countries, reflecting his rich life experiences and unwavering passion for art.

Achievements

As a coxman, his adventurous spirit led him to navigate from Romania to the far reaches of India in the east and Brazil in the west.

His dedication and skills as a head of a canteen unit were recognised when the unit achieved second place at the national level in a canteen units competition organised by the General Union of Trade Unions.
